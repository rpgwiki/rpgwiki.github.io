Trollhound


3e

         
   TN   
         

Pathfinder facts

  • CR: 3
  • XP: 800
  • Size: Medium
  • Type: Magical Beast
  • Initiative: +5
  • Senses: darkvision 60 ft., low-light vision, scent
  • AC: 15, touch 11, flat-footed 14 (+1 Dex, +4 natural)
  • HP: 30 (4d10+8); regeneration 3 (acid or fire)
  • Saves: Fort +6, Ref +5, Will +1
  • Speed: 40 ft.
  • Melee: bite +8 (1d10+6 plus disease and trip)
  • Abilities: Str 18, Dex 13, Con 15, Int 2, Wis 11, Cha 6
  • Attacks: Base Atk +4; CMB +8; CMD 19 (23 vs. trip)
  • Feats: Improved Initiative, Skill Focus (Perception)
  • Skills: Perception +8, Stealth +5, Survival +1 (+5 scent tracking); Racial Modifiers +4 Survival while scent tracking
  • Organization: solitary, pair, or pack (3–8)
  • Treasure: incidental
