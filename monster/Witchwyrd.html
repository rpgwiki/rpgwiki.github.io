Pathfinder facts

  • Senses: darkvision 60 ft., detect magic

  • HP: 68 (8d10+24)
  • Saves: Fort +7, Ref +8, Will +9
  • Speed: 30 ft.
  • Melee: ranseur +11/+6 (2d4+4/×3), 2 slams +6 (1d4+1 plus grab) or
  • Space: 5 ft.
  • Reach: 5 ft. (10 ft. with ranseur)
  • Abilities: Str 16, Dex 15, Con 17, Int 18, Wis 13, Cha 20
  • Attacks: Base Atk +8; CMB +11 (+15 grapple); CMD 23
  • Feats: Deflect Arrows, Great Fortitude, Improved Initiative, Iron Will, Persuasive

  • Languages: Common, Draconic, one or more planar languages; tongues
