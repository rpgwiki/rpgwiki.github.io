Ratling


3e

         
         
      CE

Pathfinder facts

  • CR: 2
  • XP: 600
  • Size: Tiny
  • Type: Magical Beast
  • Initiative: +2
  • Senses: darkvision 60 ft., detect magic, low-light vision
  • AC: 16, touch 15, flat-footed 13 (+2 Dex, +1 dodge, +1 natural, +2 size)
  • HP: 19 (3d10+3)
  • Saves: Fort +4, Ref +5, Will +1
  • Speed: 30 ft., burrow 10 ft., climb 20 ft., swim 30 ft.
  • Melee: bite +3 (1d3–2 plus bleed 1)
  • Space: 2-1/2 ft.
  • Reach: 0 ft.
  • Abilities: Str 6, Dex 15, Con 13, Int 12, Wis 10, Cha 13
  • Attacks: Base Atk +3; CMB +3; CMD 12 (16 vs. trip)
  • Feats: Dodge, Mobility
  • Skills: Climb +6, Knowledge (planes) +4, Perception +6, Stealth +16, Swim +6
  • Languages: Aklo; speak with animals (rodents only), tongues
  • Organization: solitary or conclave (2–20 plus 2–12 dire rats and 1–4 rat swarms)
  • Treasure: standard
