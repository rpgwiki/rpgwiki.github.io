Moonflower


3e

         
   TN   
         

Pathfinder facts

  • CR: 8
  • XP: 4,800
  • Size: Huge
  • Type: Plant
  • Initiative: +4
  • Senses: darkvision 60 ft., low-light vision
  • AC: 21, touch 8, flat-footed 21 (+13 natural, –2 size)
  • HP: 104 (11d8+55); fast healing 5
  • Saves: Fort +12, Ref +3, Will +4
  • Speed: 20 ft.
  • Melee: bite +15 (2d6+9 plus grab), 2 tentacles +13 (1d8+4)
  • Space: 15 ft.
  • Reach: 15 ft.
  • Abilities: Str 28, Dex 10, Con 21, Int 5, Wis 12, Cha 17
  • Attacks: Base Atk +8; CMB +19 (+23 grapple); CMD 29 (can't be tripped)
  • Defenses: DR 10/slashing; Immune electricity, plant traits; Resist cold 10
  • Feats: Blind-Fight, Improved Initiative, Improved Sunder, Multiattack, Power Attack, Skill Focus (Stealth)
  • Skills: Perception +9, Stealth +4 (+20 in thick vegetation); Racial Modifiers+16 Stealth in thick vegetation
  • Languages: telepathy (1 mile, other moonflowers only)
  • Organization: solitary or cluster (2–8)
  • Treasure: standard
