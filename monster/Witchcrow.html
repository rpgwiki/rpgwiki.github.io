Witchcrow


3e

         
         
      CE

Pathfinder facts

  • CR: 3
  • XP: 800
  • Size: Small
  • Type: Magical Beast
  • Initiative: +3
  • Senses: darkvision 60 ft., detect magic, low-light vision
  • AC: 15, touch 15, flat-footed 11 (+3 Dex, +1 dodge, +1 size)
  • HP: 32 (5d10+5)
  • Saves: Fort +5, Ref +7, Will +4
  • Speed: 20 ft., fly 60 ft. (good)
  • Melee: 2 talons +7 (1d6+1)
  • Abilities: Str 12, Dex 17, Con 12, Int 17, Wis 16, Cha 13
  • Attacks: Base Atk +5; CMB +5 (+7 steal); CMD 19 (21 vs. steal)
  • Feats: Combat Expertise, Dodge, Flyby Attack, Improved Steal
  • Skills: Bluff +5, Fly +15, Knowledge (arcana) +6, Perception +9, Sense Motive +6, Sleight of Hand +12, Spellcraft +6, Stealth +15; Racial Modifiers +8 Sleight of Hand
  • Languages: Abyssal, Aklo, Auran, Common; speak with animals (birds only)
  • Organization: solitary, pair, covey (3-12), or murder (13-30)
  • Treasure: standard
